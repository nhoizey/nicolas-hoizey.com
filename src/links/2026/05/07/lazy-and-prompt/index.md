> The story of Chrome 148 is that Google – in the very same release – shipped the Prompt API, <mark>a feature that was met with explicit opposition</mark> from both Mozilla and WebKit, that the W3C TAG raised deep concerns about, and that the web community has been vocally and overwhelmingly against.
