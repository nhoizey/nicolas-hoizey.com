> It’s important to remember that **‘low-tier’ does not mean old**. Likewise, **‘mid-tier’ does not mean ‘a flagship from 2018’**. They are a device class in their own right and <mark>it’s perfectly possible for your users to be using brand new low-tier devices</mark>. As such, we needn’t look to the past for our benchmarks.
