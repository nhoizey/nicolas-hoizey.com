> […] I’m a fan of physical media. You buy the work once, you get a nice little souvenir to enjoy, lend to a friend or sell to someone else. As long as you possess the artifact, you’re free to access its contents whenever you’d like… be it paper, tape, cartridge, disc or diskette.
> 
> That’s the idea, anyway. The reality can be surprisingly complicated.
